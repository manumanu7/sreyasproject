The Gorgeous Taj Mahal

Constructed by Shah Jahan as a symbol of love, he built the Taj Mahal for his late wife Mumtaz. This iconic structure is the crown jewel of India. Built using the finest marble, the Taj Mahal has interesting stories to tell. For all the information, book a guided tour that costs around Rs. 200 for Indian tourists. For a great experience, visit the full moon Taj-viewing experiences; it is something truly worth experiencing.

  • Location:Agra
  • Entry fee:Rs. 40 (Indians), Rs. 1000 (Foreigners)
